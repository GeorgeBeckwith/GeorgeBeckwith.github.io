About Beckwith Development

Beckwith Development was founded by George Beckwith in 2017. It was created to bring all completed, outstanding and future projects together, this website enables them to be presented in a presentable and professional way which looks good and is easy to navigate. The contact page allows viewers to get in touch and ask any necessary questions regarding completed projects, future project ideas and also any concerns or improvements that they have to offer on both the website and any of the projects.
